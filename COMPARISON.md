---

**Key Takeaways:**

- **Umbra** — Local-first, single-user, privacy-focused. Unique strengths: biometric workspace locking, dark/light mode, nested boards, per-item checklist connectors, offline-first. No cloud collaboration or export formats yet.
- **FigJam** — Best for real-time team workshops and design collaboration. Deep Figma ecosystem integration. Lacks dark mode, offline support, and nested boards.
- **Apple Freeform** — Fully free, deeply integrated with Apple ecosystem (iCloud, FaceTime, Apple Pencil). Apple-only platform, limited organizational features.
- **Milanote** — Purpose-built for creative professionals. Strong visual organization (columns, nested boards, web clipper). Limited free tier.
- **Miro** — Most feature-rich for enterprise teams. 5000+ templates, 160+ integrations, powerful facilitation tools. No dark mode, no offline, per-seat pricing.
